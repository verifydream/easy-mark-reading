# The Art of Digital Curation

Digital curation is more than just collecting; it is the **intentional architectural design** of information flows. In a world of noise, the editor becomes the architect of silence.

> "True elegance is not the absence of clutter, but the presence of focus."

### Key Principles

1. **Symmetry vs. Soul**: While grids provide order, asymmetry provides rhythm. 2. **The 1px Rule**: Avoid borders; use surface shifts to define reality. 3. **Typography as Voice**: Choose your fonts like you choose your friends—wisely and for the long haul.

The **Editorial Architect** doesn't just write; they structure experiences. Every markdown header is a foundation stone. Every list item is a structural pillar.
